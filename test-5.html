How a question moves through XIMNET.

One flow, three decisions. That keeps the experience clear for customers and gives operators a system they can explain.

  1. 01

    Read the request.

    XIMNET identifies intent, urgency, and the kind of answer the customer is looking for before it responds.

  2. 02

    Use approved context.

    It checks the content and systems you connect, then keeps the response inside the boundaries your team set.

  3. 03

    Resolve or hand off.

    If the answer is safe, it resolves the request. If not, it escalates with context so the next person starts ahead.

What changes after launch.

The goal is not to replace support. It is to keep the first answer from being random and make the next step easier to see.
